At our Kingswatch, we understand the importance of keeping your timepiece in perfect working condition. Our expert watchmakers are dedicated to providing top-notch repair and maintenance services for all types of watches, from classic mechanical to modern digital models.

We offer quick and efficient battery changes, strap and bracelet adjustments for a perfect fit, comprehensive servicing of the watch movement to maintain accuracy and longevity, and crystal replacement for scratched or broken watch crystals. We also ensure your watch remains water-resistant to the manufacturer’s specifications and restore the shine by polishing and cleaning the watch case and bracelet.

Our team consists of highly skilled, factory-trained watchmakers with years of experience. We use only genuine parts to ensure the best performance and durability.
